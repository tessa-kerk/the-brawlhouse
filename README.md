# The BrawlHouse

An interactive Brawl Stars fan hub — a speculative concept built solo to show what could exist for players in the gaps between updates.

**Live:** https://tessa-kerk.com/brawlhouse

You check in to Starr Park's staff dorms after hours. Four brawlers you can actually hold a conversation with (Gale, Janet, Griff, Mr. P), a mystery you solve by piecing their stories together, and a secret room you unlock along the way.

## What's here
- `index.html` — the landing page
- `prototype.html` — the interactive prototype (dialogue engine + fragment/secret-room logic)
- `case-study.html` — the write-up
- `brawlhouse-deck.pdf` — the full GTM strategy deck

## How it was built
Built in three days. Claude did the grunt work (build, copy scaffolding), the art was generated (Nano Banana) and the room animations with Veo 3.1. The concept, the writing in my voice, and every call were mine. AI-generated visuals — not official Supercell assets.
